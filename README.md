## 📌 Overview  
Food Bridge is a web-based platform that connects restaurants with surplus food to NGOs and needy people. The aim is to **reduce food wastage** and ensure that excess food is efficiently redistributed to those who need it.  

It acts as a bridge between food donors and receivers, promoting a more sustainable and responsible community.

---

## 🎯 Mission  
- Reduce food wastage  
- Help feed the underprivileged  
- Promote sustainability and community support  

---

## 👥 User Roles  

### 🧑‍🍳 Restaurant / Donor  
- Add surplus food details  
- Specify quantity and availability  
- Notify NGOs  

### 🤝 NGO / Needy Users  
- Browse nearby food listings  
- Request or accept food  
- Choose pickup or delivery  
- Mark food as delivered  

---

## ⚙️ Core Features  
- Authentication with multiple roles  
- Food listing and management  
- Location-based access  
- Pickup & delivery option  
- Delivery status tracking  

---

## 🚀 How It Works  
1. Restaurants upload surplus food  
2. NGOs browse and accept requests  
3. Food is picked up or delivered  
4. Delivery is marked complete  

---

## 🌍 Impact  
Food Bridge helps minimize food waste, support people in need, and build a more connected and responsible ecosystem.

---

## ❤️ Final Note  
A small step toward a future where **no food is wasted and no one goes hungry**.
